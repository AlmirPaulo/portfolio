How fast you can deliver?

I understand you might be in a hurry, but to deliver a high quality work everybody needs time. It's not reasonable to request a TEP work for 10,000 words for the day. Doesn't matter how fast I can type, TEP work also demands attention, research, sometimes a review, so don't be so frivolous and allow me the proper time.

That said, 2000-3000 words daily is a good expectation.
